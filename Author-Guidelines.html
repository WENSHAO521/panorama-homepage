Submission Protocols

Author Guidelines

General principles for preparing and submitting your manuscript to Panorama Scholarly Group journals.

Important: Journal-Specific Requirements

While Panorama Scholarly Group maintains high overarching standards, submission requirements (including reference styles, word counts, and specific scope) vary by discipline. Authors are strictly advised to consult the specific "Guide for Authors" on the individual homepage of their target journal before submission.

Find Your Journal

1. General Submission Criteria

Manuscripts submitted to PSG journals must be original, unpublished works that are not under consideration for publication elsewhere. We accept research articles, review papers, case studies, and book reviews depending on the journal's scope.

  • Language: Submissions must be in grammatically correct English or the specific language accepted by the journal (e.g., German, Chinese, etc.).
  • Format: Files should be submitted in Microsoft Word (.doc, .docx) or LaTeX formats.
  • Anonymity: For journals practicing double-blind peer review, please ensure the manuscript file is anonymized.

2. Manuscript Structure

Although specific structure may vary, a standard research article should generally include the following sections in order:

Title Page

Should include the full title, author names, affiliations, and the contact information of the corresponding author. (Submit as a separate file if blind review is required).

Abstract & Keywords

A concise abstract (typically 150–250 words) summarizing the objectives, methods, results, and conclusions. Provide 3–6 keywords for indexing purposes.

Main Text

Typically structured as Introduction, Methodology, Results, Discussion, and Conclusion.

References

Citations must be rigorous and accurate. Please note that reference styles (APA, MLA, IEEE, Chicago, etc.) differ by journal. Check the specific journal's sidebar or "Author Guide" section for the required style.

3. Ethical Compliance

Authors must adhere to high ethical standards. Any potential conflict of interest, funding sources, and statements regarding human/animal rights must be clearly disclosed. Plagiarism is strictly prohibited and will result in immediate rejection. Please refer to our Publication Ethics page for detailed policies.

4. Submission Process

All manuscripts must be submitted via the Panorama Open Journal System (OJS). Manuscripts sent via email will not be processed.

During submission, you will be required to:

  • Register an account or log in.
  • Select the appropriate journal and section.
  • Upload your manuscript and supplementary files.
  • Confirm the submission checklist.
