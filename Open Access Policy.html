Open Access Policy

Ensuring that research outputs are freely available to the global community, accelerating the pace of scientific discovery.

Panorama Scholarly Group (PSG) is dedicated to the dissemination of research to the widest possible audience. We firmly believe that free and unrestricted access to research is essential for the advancement of science and society.

Definition of Open Access

In accordance with the Budapest Open Access Initiative (BOAI) definition, Panorama Scholarly Group defines "open access" as the free availability of peer-reviewed literature on the public internet, permitting any user to read, download, copy, distribute, print, search, or link to the full texts of these articles without financial, legal, or technical barriers.

Article Processing Charges (APC)

Panorama Scholarly Group operates on a sustainable Open Access model to ensure the long-term archiving and wide dissemination of high-quality research.

1. Medical Research (Health Nexus)

We are proud to champion Diamond Open Access for our medical journals. This means there are NO Article Processing Charges (APCs) for authors and no fees for readers. All costs are subsidized by our institutional partners to promote global health equity.

2. Other Disciplines (Social Sciences, Humanities, Engineering, etc.)

To cover the costs of rigorous peer review, copyediting, typesetting, hosting, and archiving, a standard Article Processing Charge (APC) applies to accepted manuscripts. Fees vary by journal and article type.

* Please refer to the specific journal's official website ("Author Guidelines" or "APC" section) for the most up-to-date pricing, waiver policies for low-income countries, and payment details.

Licensing Options

PSG offers a range of Creative Commons licenses to suit the needs of authors and their funding bodies. Our default license encourages maximum dissemination, but we respect authors' rights to restrict commercial use.

CC BY 4.0 (Attribution) - Default

Allows others to distribute, remix, adapt, and build upon your work, even commercially, as long as they credit you for the original creation. Recommended for maximum impact.

CC BY-NC 4.0 (Attribution-NonCommercial)

Allows others to remix, adapt, and build upon your work non-commercially. New works must also acknowledge you and be non-commercial.

Other Licenses (e.g., CC BY-NC-ND)

Depending on specific journal policies or funding mandates, stricter licenses (like NoDerivatives) may be available upon request during the submission process.

Copyright Retention

Authors retain the full copyright of their work without restrictions. Upon acceptance, authors grant Panorama Scholarly Group a license to publish the article and identify itself as the original publisher.

Repository and Archiving

We actively support the long-term preservation of scholarly research. Authors are permitted and encouraged to deposit all versions of their paper (Pre-print, Accepted Manuscript, Published Version) in institutional or subject repositories immediately upon publication without embargo.
