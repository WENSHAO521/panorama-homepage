For Reviewers

Reviewer Resources

Peer reviewers are the backbone of scholarly publishing. We are dedicated to supporting our reviewers with clear guidelines and recognition.

1. The Importance of Peer Review

Peer review is essential to the integrity of scientific and academic communication. By providing critical, constructive, and unbiased feedback, reviewers help authors improve their work and ensure that only high-quality research is published. Panorama Scholarly Group upholds a rigorous double-blind peer review process for most of its journals.

2. Ethical Guidelines (COPE)

We strictly adhere to the COPE Ethical Guidelines for Peer Reviewers. Before accepting a review invitation, please ensure that you:

  • No Conflict of Interest: Do not have any competing interests (financial, institutional, collaborative, or other) with the authors or the work.
  • Confidentiality: Treat the manuscript and the review process as strictly confidential. Do not share or discuss the manuscript with others.
  • Timeliness: Only accept the invitation if you can complete the review within the specified timeframe.

3. Conducting a Review

When evaluating a manuscript, please consider the following key criteria:

Originality and Significance

Does the paper contribute new knowledge or a novel perspective to the field? Is the research question important and clearly defined?

Methodology

Are the methods appropriate, rigorous, and clearly described? Can the study be replicated based on the information provided?

Clarity and Structure

Is the argument logical and well-structured? Is the writing clear and accessible? Are the figures and tables informative?

Ethical Compliance

Does the study adhere to ethical standards regarding human or animal subjects? Is there any evidence of plagiarism or data manipulation?

4. Submitting Your Report

Reviews should be submitted through our online submission system. Your report should include:

  • Summary: A brief overview of the paper's contribution.
  • Major Comments: Significant issues related to methodology, analysis, or argumentation that must be addressed.
  • Minor Comments: Suggestions for improving clarity, style, or correcting minor errors.
  • Recommendation: Accept, Minor Revision, Major Revision, or Reject.

Please ensure your tone is constructive and professional. Avoid personal comments about the authors.

5. Reviewer Recognition

We value the hard work of our reviewers. In recognition of your contribution, Panorama Scholarly Group offers:

  • Certificates: Personalized reviewer certificates available upon request.
  • Discounts: Article Processing Charge (APC) waivers or discounts for future submissions to our journals.
  • Public Acknowledgment: Annual publication of reviewer lists (with permission).
