About Panorama

A modern academic publisher bridging German rigour with global dynamism.

Who We Are

Founded in 2025, Panorama Scholarly Group (PSG) is a forward-thinking academic publisher dedicated to the dissemination of high-quality research across the Humanities, Social Sciences, and Technology sectors.

Born from a vision to redefine scholarly communication in the digital age, PSG operates with a dual-headquarters structure in Berlin, Germany, and Hong Kong, China. This unique positioning allows us to bridge the rigorous traditions of European academia with the vibrant, fast-paced research landscape of Asia, fostering a truly global dialogue.

Academic Journals

We publish a growing portfolio of Open Access journals. Our journals adhere to strict double-blind peer review processes to ensure the validity and relevance of published work.

Scholarly Books

Beyond periodicals, PSG is committed to monograph and edited volume publishing. We provide authors with professional editorial support, ISBN registration, and global distribution channels.

Our Mission & Vision

In an era of information overload, our mission is to serve as a trusted curator of knowledge. We believe that science and humanities should be accessible to all.

  • Open Access: We champion the Open Access movement, ensuring that research findings are freely available to the global community without barriers.
  • Integrity First: We strictly follow the guidelines of the Committee on Publication Ethics (COPE).
  • Digital Acceleration: Leveraging state-of-the-art publishing technologies (OJS) to speed up the submission, review, and publication cycle.
